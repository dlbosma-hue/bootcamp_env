# Reflection

## Applying Communication Layer Principles

The main thing I took away from this lab is that building something for a stakeholder feels completely different from building something for yourself. When I was arranging the charts in Tableau, my first instinct was to include as much information as possible. But the more I added, the less clear it became. A stakeholder in a meeting does not need to see every detail. They just need to see where the model is doing well and where it is not.


## What I Would Do Differently

I would add a simple summary at the top of the dashboard, just three numbers: overall average score, best category, worst category. Something anyone can read in two seconds before looking at the charts. That way the key insight is visible immediately, without having to interpret anything.
