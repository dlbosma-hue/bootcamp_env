## What this form is for

You are enrolling in a private coaching programme at [STUDIO NAME]. As part of this programme, your coach uses an AI-assisted tool to track your progress, monitor your engagement with the programme, and flag early if your attendance or training activity is dropping off — so they can support you before things fall through.

To do this well, we ask your permission to use three types of data:

1. **Your coaching session attendance** — whether you checked in to your scheduled sessions
2. **Your general class attendance** — how often you visit the studio overall
3a. **Your wearable workout sessions** — sessions you manually start on your fitness device
3b. **Your full daily activity data** — steps, resting heart rate, and active minutes (requires 3a)

All are described in detail below. You can consent to any combination — though coaching check-in data is required for the programme to function. Wearable data is optional, and 3b requires 3a.

---

## Data type 1 — Coaching session check-in

Every time you have a scheduled coaching session, your attendance is recorded when you check in at the studio. This is used to:

- Track whether you are completing your programme as planned
- Flag to your coach if sessions are being missed, so they can reach out promptly
- Calculate a coaching engagement score that informs your coach's briefing each week

**This data is required to participate in the coaching programme.** It does not include anything about why you missed a session — just whether you attended.

---

## Data type 2 — General studio attendance

We already track your visits to the studio as part of your general membership. With your consent, this data is also fed into your coaching profile so your coach can see whether your overall engagement with the studio is changing — not just your coaching sessions.

**This is optional.** If you do not consent, your coach will only see coaching session attendance, not broader visit patterns.

---

## Data type 3a — Wearable workout session data

If you connect a compatible fitness wearable (e.g. Fitbit, Garmin), we can pull data from sessions **you manually start on your device**. This helps your coach understand your training between sessions.

Data accessed — **only for manually-initiated workout sessions:**
- Workout type (e.g. strength, cycling, running)
- Session start time and duration
- Calories burned during session
- Heart rate during the session (average and peak)

**This is optional.** The coaching programme works without it.

---

## Data type 3b — Full daily activity data (requires 3a)

If you consent to 3a and want to go further, we can also pull a daily summary from your wearable. This gives your coach a broader picture of your activity on days between sessions.

Data accessed — **daily summary only, no raw time-series:**
- Step count per day
- Active minutes per day (moderate + vigorous activity combined)
- Resting heart rate per day

We do **not** access sleep data, GPS location, continuous heart rate monitoring, passive background tracking, or any health condition or medical data.

**This is optional and requires 3a.** You can consent to 3a without 3b.

---

## How your data is handled

Your data is processed by an AI pipeline operated by our technology consultant, acting as a data processor under a formal Data Processing Agreement with [STUDIO NAME].

- Your name is replaced with a pseudonymous ID before your data enters the AI system. The AI never processes your name directly.
- Data is used solely to support your coaching programme and inform your coach's outreach. It is not sold, shared with third parties, or used for marketing.
- Check-in and attendance data is retained for the duration of your coaching programme and deleted within 30 days of programme end, unless you request earlier deletion.
- Wearable data is retained only while you are actively enrolled and have given consent. It is deleted within 48 hours of consent withdrawal.

**Legal basis:**
- Coaching check-in and general attendance data: processed on the basis of your contract with the studio (GDPR Article 6(1)(b)) and the studio's legitimate interest in programme delivery (Article 6(1)(f)).
- Wearable data (3a and 3b): classified as health data under GDPR Article 9 because it includes heart rate information. Processed only on the basis of your explicit consent (Article 9(2)(a)), given separately in the consent table below. Each tier (3a, 3b) is a separate consent — you can withdraw either independently.

---

## Your rights

You can withdraw consent for wearable data at any time without penalty — just tell a member of staff or email [studio email]. Withdrawal of wearable consent does not affect your coaching programme.

You can also request to:
- See all data we hold about you (right of access)
- Correct inaccurate data (right of rectification)
- Delete your data (right to erasure)
- Restrict processing while a complaint is reviewed (right to restriction)

Contact: [studio email] or speak to [responsible staff member].

If you are unhappy with how your request is handled, you have the right to complain to the German data protection authority: **Berliner Beauftragte für Datenschutz und Informationsfreiheit**, www.datenschutz-berlin.de.
