Project Introduction: 
What We Are Building & WhyProject StatementThis project builds an autonomous AI agent that researches media companies and evaluates them through an intersectional inclusivity lens. The agent does not just count how many women work at a company -- it looks deeper at how media organizations represent, portray, and give voice to people from marginalized groups.

The core belief behind this project is that media shapes public perception. When certain groups are consistently underrepresented, misrepresented, or portrayed only as victims, criminals, or side characters rather than experts and leaders, it reinforces real-world inequality. This agent tracks exactly that.

Who We Are Looking At: 
The agent evaluates media companies across four communities:
- Women and gender equality: representation in leadership, bylines, pay equity, coverage of women's issues
- LGBTQ+ communities: accurate and respectful coverage, representation in newsrooms, use of inclusive language
- Racial and ethnic minorities: diversity in hiring and sourcing, coverage of racial justice, avoiding stereotyping
- People with disabilities: representation in newsrooms, how disability is framed in reporting, language choices

For each community, the agent looks at four angles. 
The first is representation in bylines and leadership, which examines who is actually writing the stories and who holds positions of power within the outlet. 
The second is how victims are portrayed, asking whether marginalized people are shown only as victims or whether they also appear as experts, leaders, and full human beings. 
The third is sourcing diversity, meaning who gets quoted as an authority or expert in news stories. 
The fourth is language and framing, which looks at whether the outlet uses inclusive, respectful, and accurate language.

The Scoring System:
The agent gives each company a score using the principle: equal weight across all groups, but companies are flagged if they cause active harm -- for example if they consistently portray victims in a dehumanizing way or use harmful language about any group.
